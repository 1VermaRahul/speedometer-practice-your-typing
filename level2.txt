One of the 12 Jyotirlingas Shrines of Shiva, Somnath Temple is also the specimen of fine architecture.
Dubbed as Eternal Shrine, it is believed to be the place where Lord Krishna ended his Lila and thereafter for heavenly abode.
This legendary temple is said to have vandalized numerous times in the history
but with the help of zealot Hindu Kings the temple was rebuilt each time.
Here in this blog we bring to you facts and legends that are attached to this sacred and architecturally marvelous temple.
